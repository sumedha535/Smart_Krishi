DIAGNOSIS RESULT

Downy Mildew

Cause

High humidity and cool temperatures favor the spread of this pathogen. It spreads rapidly through water droplets and wind.

Solutions

  • Apply Copper-based spray
  • Improve air circulation
  • Remove infected debris

Prevention

  • Drip irrigation only
  • Resistant crop varieties
  • Regular field monitoring
